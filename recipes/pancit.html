Easy Pancit Noodles

Description

Pancit is a delicious meal. This dish originates from the philippines. This recipe is quick and easy and only needs 9 ingredients! This recipe includes chicken, rice noodles, cabbage, garlic, carrots, onion and don't forget the lemon! Make sure in prep to slice the carrots and cabbage thinly for nice sized bites and for even cooking. Coming in with only 369 calories per serving this recipe fits nicely in any recommended 2,000 calorie-a-day diet. Let's get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 1(12 ounce) package dried rice noodles
  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, finely diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups diced cooked chicken breast meat
  • 1 small head cabbage, thinly sliced
  • 4 carrot, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 lemons - cut into wedges, for garnish

Steps

  1. Place rice noodles in a large bowl;cover with warm water and let soften for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain and set aside.

  2. Meanwhile, heat oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-low heat. Add onion and garlic; cook and stir until onion is tender, about 3 to 5 mins. Stir in chicken, cabbage, carrots, and soy sauce. Cook until cabbage begins to soften. Toss in noodles and cook, stirring constantly, until heated through.

  3. Transfer pancit to a serving dish and garnish with lemon wedges.
